About Me:

Health Detective

Health Detective web application is designed to address the increasing health concerns related to obesity. It calculates the user's BMI by taking input of their first name, height (in centimeters), and weight (in kilograms), and then provides personalized health advice and a proposed meal plan based on the BMI category.
